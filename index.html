Soham Ray

AI Research, Sierra

sohamray19@gmail.com · Resume · GitHub · Google Scholar · LinkedIn

I'm a Senior Research Engineer at Sierra, working on evaluating and improving conversational AI agents under Karthik Narasimhan. I co-built τ²-bench — an agent benchmark featured in the GPT-5 launch and adopted by leading labs for model evaluation — and lead the voice benchmarking effort behind τ-voice.

Before Sierra I spent about three and a half years at ASAPP under Kilian Weinberger, applying LLMs to customer service — leading work on QA, summarization, retrieval, and human-in-the-loop systems. I have an MS in Computer Science from Cornell. Based in New York. Broadly interested in ML, NLP, and turning research into shipped systems. Outside work I read across economics, medicine, physics, and history.

Selected Work

  • τ²-Bench Sierra · 2025

    Benchmarking framework for evaluating foundation models' agentic capabilities in interactive customer-service domains where users can also take actions. Featured in the GPT-5 launch keynote; adopted by OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Moonshot AI, and Alibaba in subsequent model releases.

  • τ-voice Sierra · 2026

    First benchmark for real-time, full-duplex voice agents on real customer-service tasks. 278 tasks shared with τ²-bench enable direct voice↔text comparison under realistic acoustic conditions — noise, telephony compression, accents, and free-form turn-taking.

  • LLM Chatbot Architecture ASAPP · 2023–2024

    Designed and led a graph-based architecture for ASAPP's LLM-powered chatbot, transitioning from monolithic prompts to task-targeted agentic prompts for better configurability and robustness.

  • QA & Summarization System ASAPP · 2023

    Led a team building ASAPP's QA system: trained models for binary disposition forms and post-call summaries. Finetuned open-source models from 3B to 70B parameters, surpassing GPT-4 on human-annotated tasks.

  • Next-Utterance Recommendation ASAPP · 2022–2023

    Led research and production adoption of generative language models for ASAPP's primary chat-augmentation product; +10% usage on average across customers in A/B tests.

  • Agent Coaching System ASAPP · 2022

    Live response-suggestion system for human agents using FAISS-backed retrieval, raising response quality by 17% on human annotation by surfacing senior-agent responses in-conversation.
