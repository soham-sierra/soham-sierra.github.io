Soham Ray

Senior Research Engineer, Sierra

sohamray19@gmail.com · GitHub · Google Scholar · LinkedIn

I'm a Senior Research Engineer at Sierra, working on evaluating and improving conversational AI agents under Karthik Narasimhan. I co-built τ²-bench — an agent benchmark featured in the GPT-5 launch and adopted by leading labs — and currently lead the team building μ-bench, an open multilingual transcription benchmark for voice agents.

Before Sierra I spent about three and a half years at ASAPP under Kilian Weinberger, applying LLMs to customer service — leading work on QA, summarization, retrieval, and human-in-the-loop systems. I have an MS in Computer Science from Cornell. Based in New York. Broadly interested in ML, NLP, and turning research into shipped systems. Outside work I read across economics, medicine, physics, and history.
