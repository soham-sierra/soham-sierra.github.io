Soham Ray

AI Research, Sierra

sohamray19@gmail.com · Resume · GitHub · Google Scholar · LinkedIn

I've always been fascinated with language and communication. Growing up, that took the form of lots of reading. Once I stumbled onto the fact that I was also pretty good with computers, combining the two felt natural. I've been working at the intersection of language and AI for nearly seven years now — most recently on voice and agents.

Today I work at Sierra alongside Victor Barres and Karthik Narasimhan, with a research team I'm lucky to call friends. Before Sierra I spent three and a half years at ASAPP under Kilian Weinberger and SRK Branavan — two of the most fundamental influences on my career. It's been a fun ride from pre-GPT-3 retrieval and generative models to post-GPT agents.

Speaking of fundamental influences, I'm married to the inspiring Aashna Banerjee, and we live in San Francisco. If you're ever in SF and up for a game of squash or a boba, let me know.
